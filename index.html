About me

Hi! I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Industrial and Operations Engineering (IOE) at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, where I am very fortunate to be advised by Professor Cong Shi. My current research interests lie broadly in learning algorithms and data-driven optimization. Specific topics include in sequential decision-making under uncertainty and online reinforcement learninng algorithms, especially their applications in supply chain management and revenue management.
Before joining Umich, I studied Industrial Engineering at Tsinghua University.
