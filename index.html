About me

Jingwen Tang

 

Hi! I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Industrial and Operations Engineering (IOE) at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, where I am extremely fortunate to be advised by Professor Cong Shi. My current research interests lie broadly in learning algorithms and data-driven optimization. Specific topics include sequential decision-making under uncertainty and online and offline learning algorithms, especially their applications in supply chain management and revenue management.
Before joining the University of Michigan, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering from Tsinghua University.
